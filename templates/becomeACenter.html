Application Stage 1 - Prospective centet (customer) requests for Application forms from Computer Society of Zimbabwe (CSZ) either physically, by phone (04) 250489 or email to info@csz.org.zw Application Stage 2 - The customer fills in forms and returns them together with the required documentation and application fees. Review Stage - CSZ then contacts customer to arrange an on-site inspection of premises/hardware after which CSZ will accept/decline/advise on amendments. Status will be advised within 3 working days Accreditation Stage - If application is successful, the customer will be formally advised and a start-up pack should be purchased by the customer. Implementation Stage - Customer can start training and testing.
